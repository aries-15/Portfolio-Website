About Me

I'm a first-year college student who is just beginning my journey into the world of technology and creativity. I love exploring new ideas, learning different skills, and trying out things that help me grow both personally and academically.


I'm currently learning the basics of programming, web development, and computer science. I enjoy building small projects, experimenting with HTML and CSS, and improving my logical thinking through coding exercises. Even though I'm still at the beginner stage, I'm enthusiastic about developing my skills and finding out which area of tech interests me the most.


My goal is to grow as a developer and contribute to impactful and creative digital solutions.



Outside of college and coding, I have a very creative side. I love reading books, especially fiction and self-growth genres that inspire me or help me escape into another world for a while. I also enjoy painting and drawing, which allow me to express myself and relax after a long day. Creativity is something that keeps me balanced - whether it's art, colors, or designing something new.



This website is a small reflection of who I am - a mix of my learning journey, creativity, interests, and everything I am exploring at this stage of my life.
